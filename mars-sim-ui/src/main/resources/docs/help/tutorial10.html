Tutorial - Step 10 : Science Tool

The Science Tool is where you oversee the various research projects that your scientists are conducting.

It shows a list of on-going and finished Scientific Studies (its subject, level and phase).

If you click on a particular study, it will display the detail on the right panel showing also the primary researcher and collaborators and its percentage of completion in the progress bar.

Currently, all scientific studies are generated automatically by mars-sim.

Note: in future, users may create a plan of study for the researchers and upon completion, some may have immediate application in the health of the settlement and the impact to its inhabitants.
