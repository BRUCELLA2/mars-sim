Simulation Concepts

Below are some main concepts and terminologies used in mars-sim :



    Three types of structures on Mars :
  • Settlements
  • Buildings
  • Construction Sites
    • Note : Landmarks are currently unexplorable


    Two types of activities undertaken by a settler :
  • Missions
  • Tasks


    • Each settler has well-defined :
  • Job
  • Role
  • Personality
  • Natural Attributes
  • Preferences
  • Favorites
  • Skills


    • Two main types of objects :
  • Parts
  • Resources
    • Note 1: Crops are considered resources
    Note 2: meals are food resources derived from crops


    Three types of equipment :
  • Vehicles
  • EVA Suits
  • Robots


    • Three types of processes :
  • Manufacturing Processes for assembling more complicated items from parts and resources
  • Food Productions for producing food items
  • Resource Processes, for generating oxygen, methane, grey and black waste waters, etc..
