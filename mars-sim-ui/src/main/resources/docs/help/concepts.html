Simulation Concepts

Mars Simulation Project is a Java-based open source project that simulates the activities of the first generation of settlers on Mars.

Modeling and Simulation

As a social simulation, mars-sim strives to create credible abstract computer models of a settler's career type, job role, technical skills, personality/temperament and relationship with one another as they go about their daily lives.

Each agent has various human-like characteristics, capable of making distinct decisions and interacting with one another and perform taks and mission within the settlement and on the surface of Mars. See Modeling for details.

On a powerful machine, player may create numerous settlements spreading across Mars, from a simple 4-person initial landing site, or a mining site to more matured bases with buildings hosting various functions.

Each settler is associated with a particular settlement. Each settlement has buildings and vehicles as assets, a leadership structure (i.e. commander, sub-commander, chiefs and specialists) and a stated goal or objective which will cause it to specialize in a particular kind of outputs.

Settlements trade goods and network with one another. In each trade mission, goods are exchanged and Value Points (VP) are calculated for the two involving settlements. VP is the proto-currency system used in mars-sim. Each resource worths a certain amount of VP and the VP of each resource is dynamic and changes over time, based on the demand and availability of that resource.

Architecture

mars-sim is composed of the following constructs or archetypes :

A. 4 types of Unit

  • Structure
  • Vehicle
  • Person
  • Equipment

B. 3 types of Structure

  • Settlements
  • Buildings
  • Construction Sites
Note : Landmarks are currently unexplorable

C. 8 types of Equipment

  • Vehicles
  • EVA Suit
  • Specimen Container
  • Bag
  • Large Bag
  • Barrel
  • Gas Canister
  • Building Kit
  • Robots

D. 3 categories of Supplies

  • Buildings
  • Vehicles
  • Equipment
  • Resources
  • Parts

E. 2 types of Resources

  • Item Resources
  • Amount Resources

F. 1 type of Item Resources

  • Parts

G. Various types of Amount Resources

  • Gas, Liquid, Solid
  • Cooking Condiment
  • Chemical Compounds
  • Mineral Concentration
  • Building/Construction Material
  • Drugs/Supplements
  • Bio-organism
  • Crops
  • Utility items from crop harvest
  • Derived food resources from crop harvest
  • Wastes
Note 1: Meals are not Amount Resources but Java Objects made up of various Amount Resources

H. 3 types of Processes

  • Manufacturing Processes for fabricating items and complicated parts, assembling equipment/vehicles.
  • Food Productions for producing derived food products as well as non-edible items/resources for daily use.
  • Resource Processes, for generating gases and liquids and solids.

I. 2 types of Ground Vehicle

  • Light Utility Vehicle (LUV)
  • Rover

J. 3 types of Rover

  • Cargo Rover
  • Explorer Rover
  • Transport Rover

K. Profile of a settler

  • Job
  • Role
  • Personality : by Five Factor Model(FFM) and MBTI models
  • Natural Attributes
  • Preference : an afinity score on each of the 40+ available Tasks
  • Favorite Activities : 8 available types
  • Choice of Meals : 12+ available types
  • Skills

L. 2 types of Activities

  • Missions : 12 available types
  • Tasks : 40+ available types
