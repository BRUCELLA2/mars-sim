1. Mass Per Unit : 10.0kg

2. Description :

  • This apparatus can separate isotopes and molecular fragments according to mass. The sample is vaporized and ionized, and the ions are accelerated in an electric field and deflected by a magnetic field into a curved trajectory that gives a distinctive mass spectrum.
