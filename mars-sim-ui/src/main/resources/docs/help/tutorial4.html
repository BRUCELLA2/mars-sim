Tutorial - Step 4 : Mars Navigator Tool

The Mars Navigator is basically a constantly updating overview map of Mars and your activities on the surface.

Simply use your mouse to drag around the area inside the detailed map.

You may also drag the Mars Globe around in order to zoom in a different zoomed-in area.

In the detailed map, you may see various names of settlements, landmarks, vehicles and vehicles.

There are a total of 8 data layers that you may toggle on/off on the detailed map.

Note : since the day/night tracking layer consumes a lot of processing power to obtain the correct data points for simulating the day/night coverage of Mars, users may consider unchecking it in order to lower the cpu usage.

Currently, there are 10 available minerals that settlers may be able to prospect and mine.

You may toggle on/off the appearance of each mineral on the detailed map.

To see the info of a base or a vehicle, click on its overlaying label on the detailed map and its Unit Window will be opened.
