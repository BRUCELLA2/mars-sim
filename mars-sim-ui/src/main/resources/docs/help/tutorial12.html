Tutorial - Step 12 : MarsNet

MarsNet is a chat box located at the left end of the Status Bar.

It is a new, experimental feature that allows users to interact with the 'A.I. agent' that inhabits the MarsNet, a smart globalnet on future Mars.

You may click the MarNet button to open a chat box.

In the textfield, enter the name of a settlement, a person or a bot and choose one from the drop-down box. Next, click Enter or the 'Broadcast' button to initiate your query.

Once the connection has been established, choose a number from 0 to 15 to obtain the following info :



0: exit the conversation
1: Where is your current location (or are you inside a particular building) ?
2: What are you doing (or what task are you performing) ?
3: Where do you stay (or where is your designated bed, in what building and at which settlement) ?
4: Where are you located at (in a settlement or a vehicle) ?
5: What is your associated settlement ?
6: Are you inside a building, outside the settlement and/or on a vehicle ?
7: Which vehicle are you on ?
8: What building are you at ?
9: Which settlement are you at ?
10: What is your associated settlement ?
11: Which settlement was he/she buried?
12: Where is the location of the vehicle that you are in ?
13: Which settlement does the vehicle that you are at belong to ?
14: Is the vehicle you are in still inside a garage? Which garage ?
15: Does the vehicle you are in currently park at a settlement ? Which settlement ?

Note : you may type '0' or 'quit' or 'bye' or 'exit' and hit enter to leave the conversation.

Congratulations! You're done with the tutorial and now you know your way around mars-sim.
